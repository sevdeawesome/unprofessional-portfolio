High Seas Modpack
High Seas was an online pirate themed modpack with a custom world that a couple of friends and I created. We wrote the map to generate as 85-95% ocean dotted with small islands. The latest version can be seen here and the trailer here

Sev's LOTR Online

This modpack was a Lord of The Rings themed minecraft modpack with a built in server. Players could choose between multiple factions (Gondor, Rohan, Mordor, Gundabad, etc..) and wage massive wars on my combat based server. I didn't create the LOTR mod, this was just my rendition of it. My server quickly became one of the most popular servers with the mod and we had to limit the number of concurrent players allowed on because of how CPU demanding the mod is.
